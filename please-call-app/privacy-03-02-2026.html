Privacy Policy

Last updated: 03 February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the PCM Please Call Me app ("the App") handles user information.

Information Collection and Use

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The App is designed to provide a simple shortcut to send a "Please Call Me" request using your mobile network's USSD service.

Permissions

The App may request permission to make phone calls or open the phone dialer. This permission is used only to send the USSD code when the user taps a button.

The App does not access contacts, messages, location, or any other personal data.

Data Sharing

The App does not share any data with third parties.

Internet Usage

The App does not use the internet and does not transmit any data online.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
